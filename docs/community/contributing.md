# Contributing to La Feria CR

**Status:** 🟡 Draft · _Last updated: 2026-06-30_

> This is a **community page**. The hours and locations are only as good as the people who keep them
> updated — that means you! This guide explains, in plain language, how to help. (Esta es una página
> comunitaria; gracias por ayudar a mantenerla al día.)

You do **not** need to be technical to help. Most actions take a few taps on your phone.

## Ways to help

### 1. Suggest an edit (no account needed)
See wrong or missing **hours** or **location**? Tap **"Suggest an edit"** on the market, enter the
correct info, and submit. That's it — your suggestion goes into "needs confirmation".

### 2. Confirm what others suggested (account needed)
When someone suggests a change, it needs a few people to **confirm** it before it becomes
**Verified**. Sign in (Google or email code), find a suggestion, and tap **Confirm** if you know it's
right — or **Reject** if it's wrong. Once enough people confirm, it's marked **Verified**
automatically.

> **Why an account to confirm?** It keeps the "Verified" badge trustworthy and stops one person from
> faking many votes. Suggesting an edit stays open to everyone.

### 3. Add a market we're missing (no account needed to submit)
Know a feria that isn't listed? Tap **"Add a market"**, give its name, days, hours, and drop a pin on
the map (or use your current location while you're there). New markets start as **unverified** until
the community confirms them.

### 4. Report a problem
Something offensive, fake, or spammy? Tap **Report**. Our Community Safety volunteers review reports
and can remove bad content.

## What the badges mean
- ✅ **Verified** — confirmed by the community (or from the official 2026 list).
- 🕓 **Needs confirmation** — suggested, waiting for people to confirm.
- 🏛️ **Official** — from the official June 2026 list.
- 👥 **Community-added** — added by users like you.
- **Last updated** — when the info last changed.

## Tips for good data
- Be specific with hours (e.g. **5:00 am – 3:00 pm**, not "morning").
- Place the pin **at the entrance** where people actually arrive.
- Only add info you've **seen yourself** or know to be true.
- If you're unsure, **confirm** existing info rather than adding conflicting info.

## Good conduct
Be respectful and honest. Don't add ads, fake markets, personal data, or offensive content. See the
[Content Guidelines](content-guidelines.md). Accessibility commitments are in
[../accessibility.md](../accessibility.md).

Thank you for keeping Costa Rica's ferias easy to find. 🥬🍍
