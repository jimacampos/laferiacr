# Content Guidelines — La Feria CR

**Status:** 🟡 Draft · _Last updated: 2026-06-30_

These guidelines keep La Feria CR useful, trustworthy, and welcoming. They apply to all
contributions: suggested edits, new-market submissions, reports, display names, and (later) photos.
Enforcement roles and tooling are in [../architecture/rbac.md](../architecture/rbac.md) and
[../architecture/moderation-trust.md](../architecture/moderation-trust.md).

## What belongs here
- **Accurate market facts:** names, days, hours, locations/pins, organizer info that is public.
- **Real ferias del agricultor** and community markets in Costa Rica.
- Information you have **seen yourself** or otherwise know to be true.

## Not allowed
- **False or misleading** info; fake or non-existent markets; vandalism.
- **Spam or advertising** unrelated to a market.
- **Personal/private data** about individuals (phone numbers of private people, home addresses, etc.).
- **Offensive content:** harassment, hate speech, threats, sexual or violent content.
- **Illegal content** or anything you don't have the rights to share (incl. copyrighted photos).
- Impersonation of organizers, officials, or other users.

## Photos (Phase 8, when enabled)
- Must show the **market, stalls, or products** — not identifiable people as the subject without reason.
- No offensive, copyrighted, or unrelated images. Location metadata (GPS/EXIF) is stripped on upload.
- Screened by automated content safety **and** human review.

## Moderation actions
Community Safety and Super Admin may, in proportion to the issue:
1. **Reject / remove** a contribution or photo.
2. **Hide or disable** a market (e.g. duplicate, fake, or unsafe).
3. **Revert** a change to a previous verified value.
4. **Temporarily ban** a user who repeatedly violates the rules.
5. **Escalate** to Super Admin for overrides or role changes.

All actions are **logged and reversible** (audit trail). Automation may auto-quarantine heavily
reported content pending review.

## Reporting
Anyone can **Report** a market or suggestion. Tell us briefly why (wrong, fake, spam, offensive).
Reports go to the moderation queue; we review and act or dismiss.

## Appeals
If you believe a moderation action was a mistake, you can **appeal**. A different moderator or a Super
Admin reviews the case. Decisions and outcomes are recorded. (Appeal channel/UX to be finalized with
the moderation tooling.)

## Conduct
Assume good faith, be respectful, and prefer **confirming** good data over edit-warring. Persistent
bad-faith behavior leads to bans.

## Open questions
- Exact appeal channel and SLA.
- Auto-quarantine threshold (number of reports before auto-hide).
- Whether display names are shown publicly on contributions.
